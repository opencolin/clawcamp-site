What to Expect

A Full Day of Building

ClawCamp Oakland is a hands-on, all-skill-levels event for anyone curious about personal AI agents. Workshops, expert speakers, build challenges, and community — all in one day.

Beginner

Leave with your own personal AI agent and powerful skills to achieve almost any goal.

Intermediate

Dive deeper into agent management, multi-agent workflows, security, and token optimization.

Advanced

Explore nuanced skills, push the boundaries of what agents can do, and support those just getting started.
