About ClawCamp

ClawCamp hosts events where organisations, experts, and mentors introduce and provide the tools, training, and support people need to advance along their Personal Agent journeys. Beginners acquire personal agents and powerful skills to achieve almost any goal, while Intermediate and Advanced attendees dive deeper into the more nuanced aspects of agent management, multi-agent workflows, security, and token optimisation.
