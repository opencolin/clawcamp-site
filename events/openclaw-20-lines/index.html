About This Event

Build an Agent in 20 Lines

This hands-on build night is your chance to create a self-improving AI agent from scratch — in just 20 lines of code. Guided by Will Sentance, founder of Codesmith (ranked #1 AI training company by Forbes 2026), you'll learn the core patterns that make agents work.

Presented in partnership with Civic, this workshop strips away the complexity and gets straight to the fundamentals. Whether you've never built an agent or you've shipped dozens, this session will sharpen your understanding of what makes agents tick — and give you a working prototype to take home.
