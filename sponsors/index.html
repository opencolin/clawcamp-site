Ground Rules

ClawCamp activities are educational by design. Sponsors show up as contributors — teachers, tool-providers, challenge-setters — not broadcasters. The best activities are the ones attendees remember learning from.

We reserve the right to decline activities that shift the vibe from camp to conference booth.
