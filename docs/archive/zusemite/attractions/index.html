Attractions

1 Hour Away

Yosemite National Park

Nestled in the Sierra Nevada foothills, we're just an hour's drive from one of the world's most awe-inspiring landscapes. Yosemite offers fabulous hiking trails, stunning vistas, and immersive experiences for outdoor enthusiasts of every level.

Visit Yosemite

Local Water

Lakes and swimming holes in the region provide scenic beauty and recreational opportunities. Enjoy swimming, fishing, boating, and picnicking at spots that highlight the area's appeal as a destination for relaxation and outdoor adventure.

5-Minute Drive

Pet the Llamas

At Circle Home, just a 5-minute drive from camp, you can meet up to 31 llamas with unique personalities. Hands-on experiences include greeting, feeding, and leading the llamas, plus insight into Andean cosmology and spirituality.

Learn More

Moaning Caverns

One of California's largest caves features a breathtaking 240-foot vertical chamber deep in the Sierra Nevada Mountains. Visitors can rappel down the main chamber or take a guided walking tour through the eerie atmosphere and stunning natural formations.

Mountain Towns

Charming foothill towns with boutique shops, cozy cafes, and rich gold rush history are just a short drive away.

Wineries

Local wineries offer scenic vineyards, exquisite wines, and welcoming hospitality. Enjoy tastings, tours, and events while taking in stunning views and artisan food pairings that celebrate the region's unique terroir.

Casinos

Two casinos within 20 minutes of the property offer entertainment, gaming, and nightlife. Enjoy a variety of gaming options, live entertainment, and dining experiences for a fun night out.

Mountain Biking

Dodge Ridge Mountain offers trails for all skill levels, from adrenaline-pumping downhill descents to scenic cross-country tracks. Diverse terrain, stunning views, and well-maintained trails make this a premier riding destination.
