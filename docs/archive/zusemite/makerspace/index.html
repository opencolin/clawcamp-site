MakerSpace

The Builder Hub

The Beating Heart of Camp

MakerSpace is the hands-on hub where creators, builders, and visionaries come together to shape the future of Ethereum. Think of it as a co-working space crossed with a hardware lab crossed with a hackathon floor — all under the pines.

Open 24/7 during the festival. Bring your laptop, grab a coffee, and start building.

What's Inside

Tools & Resources

💻

Dev Stations

Monitors, power strips, standing desks, and blazing fast WiFi. Everything you need to ship code comfortably.

🧰

Hardware Lab

Raspberry Pis, Arduinos, sensors, 3D printers, and soldering stations for hardware hackers and IoT builders.

💡

Whiteboard Walls

Full-wall whiteboards for architecture diagrams, brainstorming sessions, and late-night protocol design.

🍔

Fuel Station

Coffee, snacks, and energy drinks available around the clock. Builders don't stop, and neither does the fuel.

👥

Mentor Hours

Scheduled office hours with experienced builders, protocol devs, and industry veterans. Get unstuck fast.

🏆

Demo Stage

A dedicated demo area where teams can present works-in-progress, get feedback, and iterate in real time.

Build Together

The MakerSpace isn't just about tools — it's about people. Find your next co-founder at 2AM over shared code. Get your PR reviewed by someone who just gave a main-stage talk. Teach a newcomer and learn something yourself.

The best projects at ETH Yosemite start with a conversation in MakerSpace and end with a demo on the main stage.
