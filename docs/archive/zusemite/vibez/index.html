ETH Yosemite Presents

VIBEZ

The Sound Sanctuary

Where Sound Meets the Sierra

Vibez is our immersive audio experience where ambient textures, vinyl-rooted grooves, and exploratory electronics converge beneath the pines. No velvet ropes, no bottle service. Just sound, stars, and the mountain air.

From poolside sunset sets to late-night A-Frame sessions, every corner of camp becomes a stage.
