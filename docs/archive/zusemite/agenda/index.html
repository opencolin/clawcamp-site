ETH Yosemite Festival

Agenda

Three days of talks, workshops, hackathons, music, and adventure. Here's what each day looks like.

9:00 AM

Gates Open & Check-In

Claim your cabin, tent, or lodge room. Get your wristband. Main Gate

11:00

Camp Tour & Orientation

Walk the 132 acres. Find the pools, MakerSpace, stages, and secret spots. Base Camp

12:30

Lunch

Catered meal at the Lodge Dining Hall. Lodge

2:00 PM

Opening Ceremony

Welcome address, festival overview, and community introductions. Amphitheater

3:30

Hackathon Kickoff

Teams form, bounties announced, MakerSpace opens. 48-hour clock starts. Event Center

4:30

Camp BUIDL: Session 1

Smart Contracts 101 or DeFi Architecture (choose your track). Pavilion

6:00

Dinner & Free Time

Pool, hot tub, archery, hiking, or just decompress. Camp-wide

8:00 PM

Opening Night Vibez Set

DJ set at the A-Frame with sunset pool session warmup. A-Frame

9:00

Poker Night: Tournament 1

Free-to-play Texas Hold'em. Walk-ups welcome. Lodge

7:00 AM

Sunrise Yoga & Cold Plunge

Start the day right. Guided session by the pool. Sunset Pool

8:30

Breakfast

Fueled by the espresso bar and catered breakfast. Lodge

10:00

Main Stage: Talk Block 1

3 talks on L2 Scaling, ZK Proofs, and Oracle Networks. 20 min each. Event Center

11:30

Fireside Chat: The Future of Ethereum

Panel discussion around the bonfire pit. Great Lawn

12:30

Lunch

Lodge

2:00 PM

Yosemite Day Trip (Optional)

Guided group excursion to Yosemite National Park. Returns by 6PM. Off-site

2:00

Camp BUIDL: Session 2

AI Agents workshop or Full-Stack dApp building. Pavilion

4:00

Podcast Lounge: Live Recording

Drop-in interviews and live shows all afternoon. Media Lounge

5:30

Art Gallery Opening

Curated walk through installations across camp. Camp-wide

7:00 PM

Dinner

Lodge

9:00

Vibez: Late Night Sessions

Deep house, ambient sets, and bonfire acoustics till late. A-Frame + Pool

9:00

Poker Night: Tournament 2

Lodge

7:00 AM

Morning Yoga

Sunset Pool

Main Stage: Talk Block 2

Governance, On-Chain Art, and Smart Contract Security. Event Center

Hackathon Demos & Judging

Lunch & Awards

Hackathon winners, poker champion, and community awards announced. Lodge
