Amenities

Pools & Water Features

Take the plunge in our large swimming pools, brave the cold plunge for an invigorating reset, or let the kids loose on the water slide. The Big Swim at Base Camp and the Sunset Pool at Edge Camp offer distinctly different vibes for every mood.

Fire Pits

The fire pit is the heart of camp life. As night falls, gather under the stars for stories, songs, and the kind of conversations that only happen around a crackling fire. Multiple fire pits across the property ensure there's always a warm glow to gather around.

Espresso Bar

Start your morning right with craft coffee drinks and fresh snacks at our on-site espresso bar. Whether you need a double shot before your morning hike or a slow pour while watching the sunrise, we've got you covered.

Fireplaces

Indoor fireplaces throughout the lodge and houses provide warmth and ambiance during cooler evenings. Settle into a plush chair with a book, or gather for intimate conversations in one of our many fireside lounges.

Hot Tub

Unwind after a day of adventure in our hot tub, complete with soothing jets and stunning views of the surrounding forest. The perfect way to end any day at camp.

Sauna

Our sauna is a sanctuary for unwinding. Promote circulation, support detoxification, and release muscle tension in the gentle heat. Follow it up with a cold plunge for the ultimate recovery experience.
