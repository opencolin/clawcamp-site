Podcast & Media Lounge

Connect. Create. Collaborate.

Your Studio in the Wild

The Podcast & Media Lounge is a fully equipped content creation space nestled into the heart of camp. Whether you're recording a solo episode, interviewing a builder you just met, or livestreaming a panel discussion — we provide all the technology. Just drop in.

No booking required. No setup needed. Walk in with an idea, walk out with content.

What's Included

Professional Equipment Provided

🎤

Podcast Mics

Professional condenser and dynamic mics with pop filters, stands, and audio interfaces for crystal-clear recording.

🎥

Video Setup

Multi-camera setup with ring lights, backdrop options, and HDMI capture for high-quality video content.

🎧

Monitoring

Studio headphones, nearfield monitors, and a mixing console so you can hear every detail in real time.

💻

Editing Stations

Laptops loaded with audio and video editing software. Cut, mix, and publish before you leave the lounge.

📡

Livestream Ready

Wired internet, streaming software, and multi-platform broadcasting tools. Go live to Twitter, YouTube, or Twitch.

☕

Lounge Vibes

Comfortable seating, espresso on tap, and acoustic treatment. A space designed for great conversations.

How It Works

1. Walk In — The lounge is open all day during the festival. No appointment needed.

2. Set Up — Our team helps you get mic'd up and camera-ready in minutes.

3. Record — Hit record. Interview a friend, riff on a topic, or do a live show.

4. Ship — Edit on-site or take raw files with you. Your content, your way.

Got a Show Idea?

Whether you're a seasoned podcaster or recording for the first time, the lounge is yours. Just show up.
