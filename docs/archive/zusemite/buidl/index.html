Camp BUIDL

From Curious to Proficient in 3 Days

Learn by Building

Camp BUIDL is an intensive 3-day hands-on training program designed to transform curious explorers into proficient Web3 builders. No slides. No theory-only lectures. You'll ship real code, deploy real contracts, and build real things — with mentors by your side.

Whether you're writing your first smart contract or architecting a DeFi protocol, there's a track for you.

The Program

Three Pillars

📚

Learning & Education

Structured curriculum tracks covering Solidity, DeFi, NFTs, L2s, and agent frameworks. Led by experienced instructors.

🛠

Hands-On Experience

Every session ends with a build. Deploy contracts, spin up frontends, and test your code in real-time with live mentorship.

🤝

Community Engagement

Build alongside your cohort. Form teams, share wins, and grow your network. The friendships forged here outlast the code.

What You'll Learn

Curriculum

Track 1: Smart Contracts & Solidity

▼

Start from zero or level up your Solidity skills. Cover EVM fundamentals, contract design patterns, testing with Foundry, security best practices, and gas optimization. End with a deployed contract on a live testnet.

Track 2: DeFi & Protocol Design

▼

Understand AMMs, lending protocols, oracles, and yield strategies. Build a simplified DeFi primitive from scratch. Analyze real protocol architectures and discuss security considerations.

Track 3: AI Agents & Autonomous Systems

▼

Build AI agents that interact with on-chain data. Cover agent frameworks, tool-use patterns, wallet integration, and autonomous transaction execution. Ship an agent that can read, reason, and act on-chain.

Track 4: Frontend & Full-Stack dApps

▼

Connect wallets, read contract state, send transactions, and build beautiful dApp interfaces. Cover wagmi, viem, RainbowKit, and modern React patterns for Web3 frontends.
