The Vision

What is ETH Yosemite?

ETH Yosemite is where the Ethereum community unplugs from the screen and plugs into the earth. Three days on 132 acres of Sierra Nevada wilderness. No corporate stages. No lanyard energy. Just builders, artists, and thinkers gathering around fire pits, swimming pools, and whiteboards to shape the future.

We built this festival because the best ideas don't come from conference rooms. They come from midnight conversations by the bonfire. From post-hackathon swims. From poker tables and dance floors and 5AM hikes to catch the sunrise over Yosemite.

This is the festival you'll wish never ends.

Life at Camp

The Schedule

What to Expect

Day 1 — Arrival & Ignition

Check in, claim your cabin or tent, explore the grounds. Welcome ceremony at the amphitheater. First poker tournament. Opening night Vibez set at the A-Frame.

Day 2 — Build & Explore

Hackathon kicks off. Morning yoga by the pool. Main stage talks and panels. Afternoon Yosemite day trip. Sunset sessions at the pool. Late-night coding and music until dawn.

Day 3 — Ship & Celebrate

Hackathon demos and judging. Final poker championship. Closing ceremony. The legendary last-night party. Goodbyes around the bonfire.
