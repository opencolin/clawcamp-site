## Council notes

Platform PM, Community PM, MVP PM and Growth PM all proposed a chapters table with near-identical columns; I converged them onto ONE migration. The sharpest disagreement — MVP/Growth's events.chapter_slug string vs Platform/Community's chapter_id integer FK — is resolved in favor of the FK, because both Community and Platform critiqued the slug as reintroducing the exact 'indexOf(san francisco) || indexOf(sf)' city-substring drift the app does today; slug stays only as the URL key. Chapters land in v1.1 (not later) because Platform's dependency chain is correct: everything downstream (per-chapter pages, memberships, follows, RSVP auto-follow, sitemap per-chapter URLs) FKs to a chapter, so it is the spine and must precede them. I honored the Growth-critique's CRITICAL CONFLICT — v1.0's lockdown breaks the existing anon-contacts follower count at chapters/index.html:344 — by making the real public-readable aggregate count ship in the SAME release that removes the dead read, so social proof never goes dark. The Risk-critique's demand that the rls-probe become a required CI gate by v1.1 (not v2.0) is honored here, but I sided with the MVP-critique against Risk's full Playwright/Lighthouse/axe wall this early: only the curl-based rls-probe + smoke gate ships now; the heavier suite is staged into later exit criteria.
