## Council notes

Two camps clashed hardest here and I split the decision on the merits of each half. On auth-gating: Platform PM and Risk PM wanted rsvps.profile_id FK'd to auth.users with login required; Growth PM, MVP PM and Community PM warned this craters conversion versus the frictionless Luma click. I sided with the growth camp on the FRONT DOOR — RSVP and follow accept anonymous email — because forcing magic-link before 'I'm going' is how you lose a 10-contact network. But I sided with Platform/Risk on the MECHANISM: the row routes through the v1.2 validating Edge Function with a honeypot, so it is not the raw leaky anon-insert pattern they rightly objected to, and optional account linkage preserves the upgrade path. Platform PM's strongest unique contribution — a real profiles table replacing the title='username:' hack (dashboard/index.html:775/808) with a UNIQUE constraint — is adopted in full; Community PM had no first-class identity row and the Platform-critique correctly flagged that RBAC/RSVP/follows need a trustworthy owner to FK to. Storage is provisioned ONCE here (Community-critique's explicit cross-mode coordination ask) so avatars now and recaps in v1.5 share one bucket with per-user/per-chapter RLS, rather than three roadmaps each standing up their own. Growth's UTM capture and post-RSVP invite prompt land here because the Growth-critique is right that they must arrive WITH the first share surface or early shares are unattributable — but the referral leaderboard/K-factor machinery stays deferred (no network to be viral with yet).
